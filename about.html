My story

I’m Tess, a UI designer and lover of the rule of thirds from Dublin, Ireland - calling Berlin home since 2016. I’ve long been fascinated by design and was drawn to User Interface Design, in particular, as it perfectly marries my love of art and aesthetics with my empathetic and organisational sides. I’m endlessly beguiled by people and a natural visual storyteller (having made several short documentary films - check them out here!) and love bringing this skill into all my creative projects & design processes. Away from the screen, I’m a serious analogue fiend & love capturing special places & moments with my Super8, Minolta SRT101 & Polaroid Spirit 600 film cameras. I’m a member of Labor Berlin analogue film collective where I’m learning film development & experimental techniques. I love to paint & connect with other artists by attending regular life drawing classes, exhibitions & meet ups - check out my visual art here. I’m also a real foodie and love trying new food and learning how to make new dishes. My latest obsession are my new rollerskates!
